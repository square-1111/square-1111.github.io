About

Welcome! I'm a Senior Year Computer Engineering student at Aligarh Muslim University. This whole revolution of Artificial Intelligence (AI) fascinates me. AI, coupled with other technologies has the power to make the world a better place. I draw inspiration from psychology to build practical systems at the interface of language, vision, learning and knowledge-graph.

Right now, I am working with Professor M. M. Sufyan Beg and Professor Misbahul Haque on Natural Language Generation.

During the summer break of 2019, I had the opportunity to work with Dr. Aditya Siripuram at IIT Hyderabad. We worked on attacking the black box image classifier and Generative Models.

Besides, I like to create animated 3D computer graphics, look for patterns and play football. I can often be found working on code projects or bingeing F.R.I.E.N.D.S., Sherlock or Tom and Jerry in my free time.
