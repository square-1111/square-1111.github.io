Welcome! I'm a Research Intern at Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems @ IISc Bangalore advised by Professor Chiranjib Bhattacharyya working on High Fidelity Image Generation. In general, I am interested in research that lies at the intersection of text and Image/3D Modeling.

In my free time I play football, fiddle around with threejs, solve rubik's cube and do photography.

Some of my current projects:

  • 3D Point Cloud Modeling: : PyTorch implementation of Learning Representations and Generative Models for 3D Point Clouds
  • Analogy Generation : Working on analogy-based story generation using multi-relational embeddings of entities and relationships.
  • Document Analysis using Graph Convolution Networks : Build a global heterogeneous graph for representation of words and documents for 20 Newsgroups dataset to classify documents.
  • Documenting Sexism in Amateur Literature : Fine-tuned ULMFiT to predict sexism in amateur writing from subreddit r/WritingPrompts.
  • Apery : Implemented Hill Climbing algorithm and transfer learning algoithm to generate artistic images.
  • Zero Shot learning and Application : A survey based project to explore resource bound reasoning and learning its applications.
